About me

I recently completed my Bachelor of Science in Statistics and am currently working as a research fellow at the ARI (Advanced Research & Innovation) where I contribute to ongoing research projects in biostatistics, data analysis, and applied statistical modeling.

I am passionate about advancing my knowledge and research skills, and I plan to pursue a PhD in Statistics or related fields. My current work focuses on applying statistical methods and computational tools to real-world datasets, and I am particularly interested in areas such as machine learning, causal inference, and bioinformatics.

I am committed to learning, collaborating on research projects, and contributing to high-quality, reproducible research. I am excited to develop my skills further and make meaningful contributions to the field of statistics and data science.
